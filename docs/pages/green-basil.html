    Industry Media Group, a rising Vancouver-based creative media agency, needed a website that showcased their mission, objectives, creative partners, through emphasis on interface and interaction design.

    RESEARCH

    I interviewed the client, creating a brief outlining their design requirements. These inputs drove the direction of our precedent research and our design direction toward interaction-centric design to emphasize the company's goals, objectives and partners.

    This led me to research interaction-centric website precedents that emphasized type and highlighted information through interaction cues.

    DESIGN SYSTEM

    My choice to stick with a gray-scale colour palette, paired alongside sleek sans-serif typeface, created an upscaled, refined visual appearance that highlighted coloured imagery and type readability.

    For components, I created a custom cursor with a text pop-up to extend interaction cues, and cards, to make learning about the agency's partners feel more interactive and personalized. For fonts, I chose Neue Haas Grotesk for its sleek and professional look, making use of it's wide range of text weights.

    WIREFRAMING

    I iterated on medium-fidelity wireframes to explore layouts and potential interactions, further refining the design direction.

    Throughout my iteration on the Partners, Contact Us, About, News and Legal pages, I focused on emphasizing imagery and content, through the utiliziation of thin lines to sub-divide and boxes to emphasize content.

    MOCKUPS

    I designed high-fidelity mockups for the Partners, About, Contact Us, News and Legal pages, applying the design system for consistency, and creating visual representations for interactions.

    I created mockups in standard web and mobile formats to communicate scalability and responsiveness to the web-developers. Additionally, my visualizations of interactions entailed mockups visualizing each step of the interaction alongside sufficient annotations.

    IMPLEMENTATION

    I continued to make design adjustments while the development team worked concurrently. I made layout, and content adjustments as necessary, up until the site's completion and implementation.

    Visit the official website of Industry Media Group at www.industrymediagroup.ca

    KEY TAKEAWAYS

    This project further expanded my understanding on interaction design, as well as my collaborative skills working within a UI/UX design team, and with external development teams.

    This was my first time creating a website with a specific focus on interactions, and it grew my skills in communicating interaction design through clear visual representations. Working under strict design constraints challenged and expanded my skills as a designer and a problem solver.
